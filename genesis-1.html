A Bible Story for Avynn · Genesis 1 · CSB

In the Beginning God Makes Everything

✦
Let's Pray Together First

"Thank You, God, for this story.
Thank You for making everything so beautiful.
Thank You for making me.
We love You so much. Amen."

A long, long time ago —
before you, before me, before anything at all —
there was only God.

And God had the most beautiful idea.
He was going to make a whole world, just full of love.

✦ Day One

God Made the Light

Everything was dark and quiet and still.
So dark you couldn't see even one tiny thing.

Then God spoke —
"Let there be light!"

And whoosh — warm, beautiful light poured in everywhere,
like the very first morning that ever was.

Say it together "God made the light — and it was good!"
✦ Day Two

God Made the Sky

God looked at the great big water everywhere.
He gently pushed some water way up high —
and between them, the most beautiful blue sky appeared.

Do you see those soft puffy clouds?
God made every single one — just floating up there.

Say it together "God made the sky — and it was good!"
✦ Day Three

God Made the Land and Flowers

God pushed the water to one side —
and dry, beautiful land appeared!

Then He whispered, "Let things grow."

And up popped flowers in red and yellow and purple,
and tall trees with sweet fruit hanging down,
and soft green grass everywhere your feet could walk.

Say it together "God made the flowers and trees — and it was good!"
✦ Day Four

God Made the Sun, Moon & Stars

God hung the big bright sun in the sky
to fill the days with warmth and light.

And for the nights, He hung the soft moon
and scattered thousands and thousands of tiny twinkling stars
all across the dark blue sky.

Every star you see tonight — God put it right there.

Say it together "God made the sun and the stars — and it was good!"
✦ Day Five

God Made the Fish & Birds

God filled the deep, sparkling ocean
with fish in every color you can imagine —
orange ones, blue ones, tiny little green ones!

And He filled the sky with beautiful birds,
soaring and swooping and singing their songs.

Every bird you hear outside — that's God singing too.

Say it together "God made the fish and the birds — and it was good!"
✦ Day Six

God Made Animals — and People!

God made lions with their golden manes,
gentle elephants, soft little bunnies —
every animal you could ever dream of!

Then He did something very special.
He made the very first people — made to look just like Him.

And one beautiful day, He made you, Avynn.
You are His very favorite idea.

Say it together "God made me — and I am SO good!"
✦ Day Seven

God Rested

God looked at everything He had made —
the sky, the stars, the flowers, the animals,
the people, and you.

And He smiled the biggest smile.

"It is very good," He said.

Then God rested.
That's why rest is so beautiful and holy —
even God took a quiet, peaceful rest.

Say it together "God made everything — and God is SO good!"
✦ Just for You

God Made You, Avynn

Out of everything God ever made —
every star, every flower, every ocean wave —

He made you on purpose.
With love. With a plan. With the biggest smile.

You are not an accident, sweet girl.
You are God's most beautiful idea.
And He is so, so happy you are here.

Say it together "God made me. God loves me. I am His!"

"In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth."

Genesis 1:1 · Christian Standard Bible
A Closing Blessing for Avynn

"God, thank You for making
this whole beautiful world.
Thank You for the sky and the stars,
the flowers and all the animals.
And most of all —
thank You for Avynn.
She is so very loved.
By You, and by me.
Amen."

Avynn says: "Amen! God made me!"
